who am i?

that's hard to say, really.

i don't mean that in the sense where i really want to sit here and make myself sound all super important and awesome, i'm really just a black furry who's into tech, cars, and music. i make my money fixing electronics but please don't ask me to fix yours unless i ask you first. definitely generic and normal please leave me alone.

my life started in middletown, connecticut where i eventually moved to the new orleans area where i still feel like an alien on another planet.

on cars, i used to drive a nissan juke named phantom. that car is actually still in the header for the time being. i had modified it slightly, mainly just interior stuff and little QOL things. i really like OEM+ builds so that's kinda where i went with that one. the juke community is awesome, and i was really sad to leave it once my juke started pissing oil and i needed something a bit more reliable. eventually i bought a 12 year old lexus gs (named ghost). 3.5L v6 spinning (what i believe) are the correct wheels, and very sharp handling. i haven't gotten to put as much money into it as i would like to, but it's definitely been getting some love. i just spent a dickton of money on a drop in screen replacement that runs android. my car can play doom.

on tech, i'm a mac user by day and a linux user also by day. i've been a mac power user for almost 10 years now, and i've also used linux since i was 12. at the moment i like linux more, but due to my heavy reliance on creative software (photoshop and final cut) most of my desktop usage stays on macOS.
don't tell me to use gimp i will rearrang your fingers.

on music, hip hop has been a huge part of my life since i was a kid listening to jay-z and kanye west with my dad. as i got older i found myself getting into artists like lupe fiasco, jid, tyler the creator, immortal technique, mos def, redveil, kendrick lamar, scHoolboy q, j. cole, and i'm going to stop before i just drop my entire itunes library here. oh yeah, itunes library. i feel like it's important to mention i also carry a (modded, i can't leave anything alone) iPod 6.5 with me everywhere i go. because i want music to be distraction free, and i get really tired of algorithms in art.
