black lives matter link



i, statera alopix fukumoto, am standing against brutality and systemic racism in the american police force... which is something i should have done a really long time ago. better late than never? a few days ago i donated money on my own, to a charity for supplying gas masks to #blacklivesmatter protesters. i did this without publicising it, mainly because i didn't want the credit for doing something i believed someone in my position should do. i am of african-american descent, and i should be taking this movement more seriously. so here i am, doing just that.

showing your support doesn't have to mean giving money, and i don't think you should donate money if it means you'll kill yourself in the process. a million voices is more powerful than a milliion dollars. you, dear reader, have a voice. no matter how small or quiet your voice is, you can still effect change in your environnment. you have the power to do good within your circle, and to do good within your communities. black lives fucking matter.
